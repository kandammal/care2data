SYNDROME: Serotonin Syndrome

DRUGS:
- SSRIs (Sertraline, Fluoxetine)
- MAOIs
- Tramadol

TRIAD:
- Neuromuscular hyperactivity
- Autonomic hyperactivity
- Altered mental status

KEY SYMPTOMS:
- Tremor
- Diaphoresis
- Confusion
- Agitation
- Tachycardia

CLINICAL ACTION:
- Stop serotonergic drugs
- Supportive care
- Cyproheptadine

SEVERITY:
Critical

SOURCE:
Hunter Criteria, FDA, PubMed
