SYNDROME: Drug-Induced Cardiac Adverse Event

KEY SYMPTOMS:
- Chest pain
- Palpitations
- Dyspnea
- Edema

MECHANISM:
QT prolongation, arrhythmogenesis, myocardial ischemia, or heart failure exacerbation.

DIAGNOSTIC TESTS:
- ECG
- Troponin
- BNP
- Echocardiogram

CLINICAL ACTION:
- Emergency ECG
- Cardiology consult
- Stop suspected drug

SEVERITY:
High

SOURCE:
AHA, FDA, PubMed
