SYNDROME: Drug-Induced Neurotoxicity

KEY SYMPTOMS:
- Confusion
- Tremor
- Dizziness
- Headache

MECHANISM:
Neurotransmitter imbalance, metabolic encephalopathy, or direct neurotoxicity.

CLINICAL ACTION:
- Neurological exam
- Electrolytes
- Stop neurotoxic agent

SEVERITY:
Moderate

SOURCE:
UpToDate, PubMed
