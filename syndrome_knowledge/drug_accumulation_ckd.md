SYNDROME: Drug Accumulation in Chronic Kidney Disease

RISK FACTORS:
- eGFR <60
- Prolonged therapy
- Elderly

PATHOPHYSIOLOGY:
Reduced renal clearance leads to toxic plasma levels.

CLINICAL ACTION:
- Dose adjustment
- Therapeutic drug monitoring
- Discontinue nephrotoxic drugs

SEVERITY:
High

SOURCE:
KDIGO Guidelines, FDA
